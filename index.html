The world’s most effective & affordable technology to fight counterfeit products

The Problem

1. Existing methods (such as special printing, Nano material, QR code, 800 toll free numbers) are not working. They are either too expensive or very vulnerable

2. Consumers want to know if their products from various brands are genuine, but there is no such a generic platform

3. Producers want to ensure their products are delivered into the customers, but they never know what has happened in between

4. Producers want to interact with their customers (such as loyalty point programs, market promotions), but there is no such a common channel or affordable system

The Solution

An end-to-end, affordable and secure solution powered by latest development in Computer Graphics, Cryptography, Blockchain, React JS. The same technology has been used in Bitcoin which has the capital market of 30 billion US dollars

The Simple steps

1. Producer: Retrieve and print the unique identity code from Lasting Stream

2. Consumers: Scan the code and verify the products’ identity without any dependence on any third party (including Lasting Stream)

3. Producers: Able to know if their products are delivered into the hands of customers, and are also able to interact with them without any dependence on any third party (including Lasting Stream)

The Application

1. Food, Wine & Spirits

2. Certificates (such degree, license etc)

3. Pharmacy

4. Electronics

5. Automotive

6. Supply Chain and finance

7. Customer loyalty program

8. Direct sales
