In Chain We Trust

The Problem

1. Existing technologies (such as special printing, QR code, 800 toll free numbers) are not working. They are either too expensive or very vulnerable

2. Consumers want to know if their products from various brands are genuine, but there is no such a generic platform

3. Producers want to ensure their products are delivered into the customers, but they never know what has happened in between

4. Producers want to interact with their customers (such as loyalty point programs, promotions), but there is no such a common channel or affordable system

The Solution

Blockchain based technology which enable the following:

1. Low cost and unbreakable due to the nature of Blockchain (same technology used by Bitcoin, LiteCoin, Ethereum)

2. Consumers can check any products’ identity from a single platform via a simple scanning

3. Producers are always able to know if their products are delivered into the hands of customers, and are also able to interact with them without any hurdles

The Application

1. Food

2. Wine & Spirits

3. Pharmacy

4. Electronics

5. Automotive

6. Supply Chain

7. Customer loyalty program

8. Direct sales
